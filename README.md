## 💡 What This Series Is About

Welcome to **The AI Systems Notebook** — a practical, long-form learning series where I break down complex AI concepts into clear, engineering-focused principles.

Instead of treating AI like a chatbot, this series explores how modern intelligent systems are actually built — from prompts and reasoning to retrieval architectures, memory systems, and autonomous agents.

It is both:
- 📚 a documentation of my learning journey
- 🛠️ a systems-focused breakdown of modern AI engineering

## 🌱 Why This Series Exists

AI system design is becoming a core part of modern software infrastructure.

Understanding how these systems work is becoming increasingly important for:
- developers
- students
- engineers
- researchers
- product builders

This series focuses on three things:

| | |
|---|---|
| 🧩 **Simplicity** | Explaining difficult AI concepts without unnecessary jargon or hype. |
| 🔗 **Connection** | Connecting conceptual intuition directly to engineering decisions. |
| 🛠️ **Application** | Understanding how reliable AI systems are actually designed and deployed. |
